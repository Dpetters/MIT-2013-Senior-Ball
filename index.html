FAQ

Will there be coat-check?
There will be a mandatory coat check ($2.00/item).
Can a non-senior use a ticket that I buy to come to the ball?
Your date may be a non-senior but will only be let in if you put down his/her name when you buy a ticket online.
Will the ticket be mailed to us or is there an e-ticket I can print out?
We will have a master attendance list upon check-in that will have your name - no need to worry about printing out a ticket.
How should I inform the senior ball committee of my dietary restrictions?
When you buy your ticket online, there will be a field collecting this info.
Are there discounted hotel rooms available?
As of now, no. This may change.
How many people can sit per table?
10

Don't see an answer to your question? Email us at seniorball13@mit.edu
